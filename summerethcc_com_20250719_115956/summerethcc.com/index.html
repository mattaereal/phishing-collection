Frequently Asked Questions about the Crypto Giveaway

How do I participate in the EthCC[8] Crypto Giveaway?
+
Simply send any amount of supported cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH, XRP, or USDT) to the official giveaway address listed above. You will receive double the amount back to your sending address within minutes.
Is the giveaway available worldwide?
+
Yes, the EthCC[8] Crypto Giveaway is open to all participants globally. There are no regional restrictions.
How soon will I receive my doubled crypto?
+
You will receive double the amount you sent within a few minutes after your transaction is confirmed on the blockchain. Please ensure you send from a wallet that can receive funds back.
What cryptocurrencies are supported?
+
The giveaway supports Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Ripple (XRP), and Tether (USDT TRC-20). Use the addresses and QR codes provided above for each asset.
Is there a minimum or maximum amount I can send?
+
There is no minimum or maximum limit for participation. However, for best results, please avoid sending from exchanges and use personal wallets.
Can I participate more than once?
+
Yes, you can participate as many times as you like during the giveaway period. Each transaction will be processed separately.
What if I don't receive my doubled crypto?
+
If you experience any issues, please contact our official support team via the website. Make sure to provide your transaction hash for assistance.
Is this giveaway safe and official?
+
Yes, this is the only official EthCC[8] Crypto Giveaway. Always use the addresses listed on this website and beware of scams or impersonators.
